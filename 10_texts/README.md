## Sample Text Structure

### Example: "The Way of a Pilgrim"

**Text**: *Откровенные рассказы странника духовному своему отцу*

**Location**: `/10_texts/intermediate/02_hagiography/way_of_pilgrim.md`

**Format**:
```markdown
# The Way of a Pilgrim (Excerpt)

## Original Text
[Russian text with stress marks]

## Vocabulary
- странник (wanderer, pilgrim)
- духовный отец (spiritual father)
- [etc.]

## Grammar Notes
- Use of imperfective aspect for habitual action
- Genitive case after "у" (possession)

## Cultural Context
- 19th century Russian spiritual classic
- Anonymous pilgrim author
- Jesus Prayer tradition

## Theological Themes
- Unceasing prayer
- Hesychasm
- Spiritual guidance

## Discussion Questions
1. What is the significance of the Jesus Prayer?
2. How does the pilgrim's journey reflect spiritual search?
```

## Reading Strategies

### Extensive Reading

**Purpose**: Comprehension, vocabulary acquisition
**Method**:
- Read for general understanding
- Don't look up every word
- Focus on overall meaning
- Build reading stamina

**Texts**: Graded readers, simplified texts

### Intensive Reading

**Purpose**: Deep understanding, language analysis
**Method**:
- Close reading
- Analyze every sentence
- Look up unknown words
- Study grammar structures

**Texts**: Literary excerpts, theological passages

### Parallel Reading

**Purpose**: Comprehension support, translation skill
**Method**:
- Russian text with English translation
- Compare versions
- Note differences
- Build intuition

**Texts**: Bilingual editions
